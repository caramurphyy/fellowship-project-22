Navigation App for Blind Client

A custom app for a blind client as a navigation guide for a low cost. To develop an interface for this app, I had to focus heavily on the user experience, to ensure our client would be able to use the app on his own.

Pictured on the right is how we intended the app to be used. A 3D attachment attaches his phone with our app running to the white cane and scans the area ahead for obstacles.
