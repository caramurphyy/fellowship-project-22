NAVIGATION APP FOR BLIND CLIENT

The landing page of the application. A large button to start the guiding process is presented and speaks when pressed. Press again to stop.
The CAD blueprint of our device used to attach the user's phone to the cane.
Photo of intended use.
How our algorithm would analyze camera feedback.

This custom app was developed for a blind client as a navigation guide for a low cost. Along with three others, I worked with the client to test and update our algorithm and interface over the course of a year.

As part of my group, I spearheaded the development of the user interface and initiated the integration of Python into our application based in Android Studio. Developing the user interface was a challenge as the focus was entirely on user experience rather than appearance.
